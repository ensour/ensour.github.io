Messaging Services Privacy Policy

Messaging Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how Jubran Restaurant ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you communicate with us through our messaging services on WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, and Instagram Direct Messages.

This policy applies specifically to our automated messaging assistant and supplements our general website privacy policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you send us a message through WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, or Instagram, we may collect:

  • Your name and profile information as provided by the messaging platform
  • Your phone number (WhatsApp) or platform user identifier (Messenger, Instagram)
  • The content of messages you send to us, including text and voice messages
  • Reservation details you provide (date, time, party size, seating preferences, special requests)
  • The date and time of your messages

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected through our messaging services to:

  • Respond to your inquiries about our menu, hours, location, and services
  • Process and manage table reservations
  • Provide customer support and assistance
  • Improve our services based on common questions and feedback

3. AI-Assisted Messaging

Our messaging service uses an AI assistant to help respond to your messages. The AI assistant processes your messages to understand your questions and provide accurate, helpful responses about our restaurant. Your messages are processed in real time and are not used to train AI models.

4. Data Retention

We retain your information as follows:

  • Conversation history: Recent messages are kept temporarily (up to 30 minutes of inactivity) to maintain context within an ongoing conversation. After this period, the conversation context is automatically deleted.
  • Reservation records: If you make a reservation, the booking details (name, date, time, party size, preferences) are stored in our reservation system until the reservation is fulfilled or cancelled.
  • Conversation logs: We may retain anonymized records of conversations for service improvement and analytics purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your messages are processed through Meta's platforms (WhatsApp, Messenger, Instagram) in accordance with Meta's Privacy Policy. We do not share your messaging data with any other external parties.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. Messages are transmitted through the encrypted channels provided by each messaging platform (WhatsApp uses end-to-end encryption). Our internal systems are protected with access controls and secure storage.

7. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data from our systems
  • Stop receiving messages from our automated assistant at any time

To request deletion of your data or exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@jubran.com or send us a message saying "delete my data" through any of our messaging channels. We will process your request within 30 days.

8. Human Escalation

If our AI assistant is unable to help you, or if you request to speak with a person, your conversation may be transferred to a member of our team. When this happens, the team member will have access to your recent conversation history to provide continuity of service.

9. Children's Privacy

Our messaging services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
