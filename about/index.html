Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor

The Beginning

Inspired by a
Poet's Spirit

Jubran Restaurant draws its name and soul from Gibran Khalil Gibran, the celebrated Lebanese-American poet, writer, and visual artist whose work transcended borders and cultures. Just as Gibran sought to bridge the East and West through art and philosophy, our kitchen bridges tradition and innovation through the language of food.

Founded on the vibrant Abdali Boulevard in Amman, Jordan, Jubran has spent over a decade cultivating an experience that honors the rich culinary heritage of the Levant while embracing contemporary techniques and presentation.

Every dish on our menu is a tribute to the generations of cooks, farmers, and artisans who shaped Levantine cuisine into one of the world's most beloved culinary traditions. We source the finest local ingredients and prepare each plate with the care and artistry it deserves.
