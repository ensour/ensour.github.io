Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor

The Beginning

Inspired by a
Poet's Spirit

Named after the renowned Lebanese poet and philosopher Gibran Khalil Gibran, Jubran reflects his timeless values of art, culture, and humanity — principles that shape every aspect of the dining experience, from ambiance and design to hospitality and culinary philosophy.

Writing in both Arabic and English, Gibran bridged cultures and audiences with a universal voice — a duality that inspires Jubran's approach to cuisine: deeply rooted in Levantine tradition, yet open, expressive, and globally refined.

Inspired by the region's diversity, Jubran's menu is thoughtfully composed to honor heritage while embracing contemporary interpretation, echoing Gibran's ability to unite ideas, cultures, and expressions into a single, harmonious narrative.

Heritage

A Legacy of Excellence

Since opening its doors over a decade ago at The Boulevard in Abdali, Jubran has established itself as one of Amman's distinguished dining destinations. Guests are drawn to its elevated cuisine, inviting atmosphere, and consistently attentive service — elements that have cultivated a loyal following and a reputation for refined taste and authenticity.

Guided by a commitment to excellence, Jubran continues to evolve, reimagining its space, refining its menu, and elevating every detail to deliver a dining experience that is both sophisticated and heartfelt.

Purpose

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver a distinctive dining experience that honors the richness of Levantine cuisine while embracing a contemporary touch — defined by exceptional quality, heartfelt hospitality, and a refined atmosphere that inspires connection, comfort, and cultural appreciation.

Our Vision

To establish Jubran as a leading regional dining destination known for its distinctive Levantine culinary experience, artistic ambiance, and unwavering quality — with plans to expand across the Middle East and beyond through strategic franchising.

The Kitchen

Masters of Flavors

With years of collective experience, our culinary team is dedicated to bringing traditional Levantine recipes to life through a contemporary lens. Guided by craftsmanship and expertise, each creation reflects a deep respect for heritage while meeting the highest standards of quality and refinement.

A shared passion for food and an unwavering commitment to excellence are evident in every meticulously crafted dish — offering our guests an immersive journey through the rich and expressive flavors of the Levant.

Ambiance

The Experience

Jubran blends modern design with Levantine charm, creating a space that is both refined and welcoming. Elegant interiors extend to open-air terraces and a rooftop overlooking the city, where Amman's skyline becomes part of the experience.

Whether visiting for a relaxed lunch or a festive evening gathering, the ambiance reflects what we value most — comfort, generosity, and togetherness.
